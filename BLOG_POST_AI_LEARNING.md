## FAQ

### Q: How many emails does the AI need to learn my style?
**A**: Minimum 3 emails to start, 50-90 for high confidence. The more emails, the better the accuracy.

### Q: Can I have different styles for different contexts?
**A**: Currently one unified style per user. Multi-persona support coming Q2 2025.

### Q: Does the AI learn from emails I write outside GetAnswers?
**A**: Yes! It analyzes your sent emails from Gmail/Outlook, regardless of how you sent them.

### Q: How often does the profile update?
**A**: Automatically refreshed daily if >30 days old. Edit patterns analyzed weekly. You can trigger manual refresh anytime.

### Q: What if I don't want AI learning enabled?
**A**: Visit `/ai-learning` and click "Delete Profile". The AI will continue working but without personalization.

### Q: Is my writing data used to train AI models?
**A**: Absolutely not. Your data is analyzed in isolation and never used for model training or shared with other users.

### Q: Can I export my learned profile?
**A**: Yes! Use the API (`GET /api/ai-learning/profile`) or contact support for a JSON export.

### Q: How much does AI learning cost you (infrastructure)?
**A**: ~$0.30 per user per month. We cache aggressively to keep costs low while providing value.
