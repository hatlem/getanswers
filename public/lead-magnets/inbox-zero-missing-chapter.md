# Inbox Zero: The Missing Chapter

## 7 advanced tactics most teams skip

---

## 1. Negative Filtering Rules
Create a rule that removes low-value noise from the top 20% of your inbox.

## 2. SLA Buckets
Tag every incoming email with a response SLA (1h, 24h, 72h).

## 3. Topic Heatmap
Track top 5 topics weekly to spot recurring gaps.

## 4. Escalation Matrix
Define when a human must intervene.

## 5. Smart Snooze Windows
Batch similar topics into twice-daily blocks.

## 6. Auto-Reply Guardrails
Block automation for sensitive language triggers.

## 7. Weekly Clean Sweep
End Friday with a backlog reset and summary.
